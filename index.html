PROTFOLIO

Sun-punch Man

In 1972, Ip Man was old and getting weaker. The day before he whistled past the graveyard, he slept on a bed with his apprentices' accompany. An apprentice can't help asking: "Why is your Wing Chun so awesome?". "Fight is not only a fight, it is a music, a rhythm. The rivals' pace, their breath, their heartbeat compose everything. If you get the rhythm deep in your heart, you are invincible." said by Ip Man while he recalled the battle with 10 Japanese soldiers when he was young...

Features:
- Unprecedented and characteristic gameplay
- Funny artworks and exquisite effects
- Spoof in a serious manner
- Becoming Ip Man and enjoying the fight with thousnads of soldiers

Eryla

Trista, a little girl living in a basement from birth. As her uncle's death, there is nobody that restrict her from going outside. She starts her journey to explore the world ruined and full of monsters with Eryla, an epic and powerful magic bow found in the basement. What is waiting for her besides the monsters?

Features:
- A fusion of RPG and rhythm game
- A new definition of a rhythm game: one is no longer only achieving a higher score but a battle
- Different combinations and synthesis of versatile skills that assist your battle
- Unprecedented and characteristic gameplay
- A vivid 3D world for exploring the worldview
- Awesome story and artworks
